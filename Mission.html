Mission

Our Mission is:

  • To execute penalties imposed by imprisonment sent through convictions or orders.
  • Providing a standardized consultative service to prisoners.
  • Color schemes and various skills training, aimed at transforming the attitude and behavior of inmates.
  • Provision of various basic supplies and services.
  • Helping inmates to lead their lives under the law as peaceful and productive citizens.
  • Assuring that inmates are responsible for protecting themselves from crime and defending themselves against crime.
